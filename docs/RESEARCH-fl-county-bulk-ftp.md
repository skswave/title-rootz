- FTP layout: miamidadeclerk.gov/library/FTP_File_Layouts/FTP_Layout_Official_Records.pdf
- Data types: Civil, Criminal, Family, Marriage, Official Records, Parking, Traffic, VAB
- Daily, weekly, monthly files available for 30 days
