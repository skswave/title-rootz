## 1. Top Farming Methodologies

### Tom Ferry — Geographic Farming
- **Farm selection**: Minimum 6% annual turnover rate. Fragmented agent market share (no dominant agent)
- **Farm size**: 250-500 homes for new agents, 500-2,500 for established
- **Frequency**: Monthly mailers minimum, 12 touches per year
- **Timeline**: First 12-18 months is pure investment. First listing at 12-18 months. By year 3, target 15-20% market share

### Brian Buffini — Referral-Based Farming
- Database-driven (150+ contacts), "pop-bys" + handwritten notes + monthly eReports
- Agents generate 70%+ of business from referrals vs. cold prospecting
- Named 2026 Real Estate Newsmaker by RISMedia

### Keller Williams BOLD — Circle Prospecting
- When one home hits market, call/knock 25-50 nearest homes
- Statistics: 3 more sellers will list within 90 days of any listing
- 36:12:3 model: 36 closed sides/yr, 12 direct contacts/day, 3 hours lead gen/day

### REDX — Micro-Farming (Emerging)
- 200-500 homes (not thousands), depth over breadth
- By end of first 90 days, contacted most homeowners 2-3 times
- Hyper-local data: average list price, DOM, sold-to-list ratio for specific neighborhood

### FORCED Seller Signals (MUST Sell)

### VOLUNTARY Seller Signals (MIGHT Sell)

| Signal | Data Source |
|--------|-----------|
| Absentee owner + high equity | Tax records / MLS |
| Long ownership tenure (10+ years) | Tax records |

| FSBO listing | MLS / signs — 87% eventually list with agent |
| Major renovation permits | Building department |

### List Stacking — THE KEY INSIGHT
Single-signal leads convert at 1-3%. **Stacked signals convert at 5-8%.**

High-probability stacks:
- Pre-foreclosure + 40%+ equity + 10yr ownership + absentee = **highest motivation**
- Divorce + high equity + long ownership = **forced sale with negotiation room**
- Probate + vacancy + out-of-state heir = **inherited property nobody is managing**
- Tax delinquent + code violations + absentee = **owner has mentally checked out**

### 120-Day Pre-Listing Window (Goliath Data)
Measurable life-event signals appear 90-120 days before listing:
- Job relocations, relationship changes, financial shifts, major renovations
- AI-enhanced CRMs report **67% conversion lift** and close deals **27% faster**

## 7. Florida Legal Compliance — CRITICAL

### FTSA (More Restrictive Than Federal TCPA)
- **Calling hours**: 8 AM - **8 PM** (one hour shorter than federal)
- **Consent**: Prior express written consent for ALL automated calls/texts
- **Penalties**: **$500 per violation**, up to **$1,500 willful**. 4-year statute of limitations
- **Private right of action**: Consumers can sue directly (class actions proliferating)
- **Florida DNC list**: Separate from federal; must comply with BOTH
- **"STOP" rule**: 15-day grace period after "STOP" to cease all contact
- **RE exemption**: Licensed agents calling in response to yard sign/advertisement are exempt

### Compliance Checklist
1. Scrub against BOTH National and Florida DNC every 31 days
2. Never autodialer/prerecorded without written consent
3. Manual-dial only during 8 AM - 8 PM ET
4. Honor "STOP" within 15 days (aim for immediate)
5. Keep consent records 4 years minimum
6. **Direct mail has NO DNC restrictions — safest farming channel**
